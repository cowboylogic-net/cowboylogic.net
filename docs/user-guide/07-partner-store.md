# 07 Partner Store

Use this section if your account has **Partner** access. (Admins can also open Partner Store.)

## Task: Open Partner Store

1. Log in.
2. Open `Partner Store` from navigation.
   - **Desktop:** `Partner Store` is in the top navigation.
   - **Mobile:** `Partner Store` is in the burger menu.

What you should see:

- A partner-only book list.
- Partner pricing shown on each book card (for example: `Partner Price` + note like `Wholesale from 5 units`).
- Stock indicator on the card (for example: `In stock: ...` or `Out of stock`).

If it doesn’t work:

- If you see `Access Denied`, your account likely does not have Partner access.
- Log out and log in again after role changes.
- Contact `<SUPPORT_EMAIL>` to confirm your role.

## Task: Sort and paginate Partner Store

1. On `Partner Store`, open the `Sort by` dropdown.
2. Choose a sorting option.
3. If there are multiple pages, use pagination controls to switch pages.

What you should see:

- The list updates to match the selected sort.
- Page number changes when you navigate pages.

If it doesn’t work:

- Refresh the page once and try again.

## Task: Add partner items to cart (wholesale rule)

1. In `Partner Store`, find the book you want.
2. Set the quantity (partner orders use a minimum quantity of **5** per item).
3. Click `Add to cart`.

What you should see:

- The item is added to your cart using partner rules (wholesale from 5 units).

If it doesn’t work:

- Make sure quantity is **5 or more**.
- If the book shows `Out of stock`, you cannot add it to cart.

## Task: Checkout a partner order

1. Open `Cart`.
2. Confirm each partner item has quantity **5 or more**.
3. Click `Checkout with Square`.

What you should see:

- Square checkout opens using your current cart items.

If it doesn’t work:

- Fix any item below minimum quantity.
- If stock validation fails, adjust quantities or remove the problem item(s) and retry.
