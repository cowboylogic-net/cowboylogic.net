# 06 Account, Profile, Favorites, Orders

This section covers your personal account pages after you log in.

## Task: Open your profile

1. Log in.
2. Open the account menu.
3. Click `My Profile`.
   - **Desktop:** `My Profile` is in the header.
   - **Mobile:** `Profile` is in the burger menu (and also available from the profile area).

What you should see:

- Your profile page with your account details.

If it doesn’t work:

- Refresh the page once.
- If you were logged out, log in again.

## Task: Update your profile details

1. Open `My Profile`.
2. To edit a field, click the `Edit` button next to that field.
3. Update the value and save (the edit mode shows Save/Cancel controls).

Fields available on the page include:

- `Full name`
- `Phone number`
- `Newsletter subscription`
- `How did you hear about us?`

What you should see:

- The updated value is shown on the page after saving.

If it doesn’t work:

- Refresh the page and check if the change stayed.
- Try updating one field at a time.

## Task: Update your avatar (profile photo)

1. Open `My Profile`.
2. Click `Change Avatar`.
3. Choose an image file from your computer.

What you should see:

- Your profile photo/avatar updates on the page.

If it doesn’t work:

- Try a different image file.
- Refresh the page after upload.

## Task: Change your password

1. Open `My Profile`.
2. In the `Change password` section, click `Change password`.
3. Enter `Current password` and `New password`.
4. Click `Save` (or `Cancel` to discard).

What you should see:

- Password change completes successfully (and the form closes or returns to view mode).

If it doesn’t work:

- Make sure your current password is correct.
- Try a different new password and save again.
- If you are still blocked, contact `<SUPPORT_EMAIL>`.

## Task: Add and view Favorites

1. Open a book in the store.
2. Click the favorites button (heart / favorite) on the book.
3. Open `Favorites` from the menu.

What you should see:

- A list of the books you saved.

If it doesn’t work:

- Make sure you are logged in (Favorites require a user account).
- Add at least one favorite book first.

## Task: View your orders

1. Open the account menu.
2. Click `My Orders` (or `Orders` on mobile).
3. Review your orders list.
4. To narrow the list, use `Filter by status`.

What you should see:

- A list of your orders with totals and statuses.

If it doesn’t work:

- After checkout, wait a moment and refresh once.
- If you still don’t see the order, confirm the payment finished on the success page.
